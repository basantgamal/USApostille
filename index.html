United States Apostille ®

United States Apostille provides a complete apostille guide from 50 Secretary of States offices and from US Department of State in Washington DC. We aim to educate the public about the Apostille procedures and how to certify documents by each state in the United States. Our guide contains Apostille information including fees, processing time, any special instructions about document preparation, and information about expedited services if necessary. Additionally, United States Apostille explains how you can submit your documents to the appropriate authority.
