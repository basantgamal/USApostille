Fedral Apostile

This is a special offer to obtain an expedited service for documents issued by federal entities.

Most common federal documents are issued by FDA, FBI, and USPTO (images are displayed on the right).
The FBI background check and the digital delivered FDA originals files can be accepted to be sent electronically by email. To use the digital processing, please visit www.eapostille.com .

All 6 other types (FDA, USDA, & USPTO) listed on the right must be mailed to:

United States Apostille

Legalization Department

1615 Bay Head Rd.

Annapolis MD 21409

Tel: 1 (888) 999-3434

The extra expedited fee is $37.00 The US Department of State fee is $8.00 Total service fee is $$45.00

Payment is accepted in the form of a company check or money order. Billing is available for registered FDA companies and law offices. Payments by credit card are subject to an extra handling fee 5%.

There are also other types of federal documents such as the Department of Treasury, Social Security (these mostly get rejected if they are copies), and judgments issued from a federal court (we require to stamp it first from the Department of Justice).

These 2 documents can be received digitally by email, or can be sent as hard copies.

The original version of these 6 documents is required. No digital files or copies are accepted.
